# Kobrain — Shelved March 28, 2026

## What It Was
**"Your AI teammate. Has an inbox. Does the work."**

An AI teammate platform — an AI agent with its own identity, inbox, memory, and persistent presence that could do work for you autonomously.

## Core Promise
- Safe agentic AI
- "Your AI. For you. Not as you."
- Has its own inbox and does the work without being prompted

## What Was Built
- Landing page (dark theme, electric blue/cyan): https://kobrain-deploy.vercel.app
- Logo: Circuit Brain (blue circuit nodes forming a brain) — see /videos folder
- Supabase backend for waitlist
- Promo videos (60s cinematic, skill cinematic, password heist ad)
- Brand assets and copy

## Why It Was Shelved
Sansar pivoted to building a personal content brand around investment analysis. The AI teammate platform requires more resources, users, and infrastructure than a one-person operation can manage while also producing content.

## Files
- Landing page repo: https://github.com/jccollinsdev/kobrain
- Logo assets: /videos/kobrain-logo-*
- Promo videos: /videos/kobrain-*

## Could Come Back
Yes — if Sansar decides to build the AI teammate platform in the future, the brand and assets are here. The landing page is still live at kobrain-deploy.vercel.app.
